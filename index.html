What it does

Everything you did.
Nothing you didn't.

TripFolio is built for one job: remembering your trips — where you went, how far, how many nights — clearly, calmly, and without phoning home.

Every stop, logged

Add each stop with its dates, nights, a place name and notes. TripFolio lays your whole route out in order, from the driveway to the last camp.

Distance by mode

Drive, fly or ferry — the kilometres are tallied per leg and totalled by mode automatically, so you can see exactly how far a year went.

Map & timeline

Every trip pinned on a world map and stacked on a year‑by‑year timeline, so the whole story is one swipe away.

Stays & ratings

Rate where you stayed, tag the type — motel, camp, other — and remember the good ones for next time.

Made to match

Light, dark or automatic, paired with seven accent colours — teal, blue, indigo, purple, rose, orange, green.

Built to share

Turn any trip into a cinematic, recap or journal card for sharing — or export your full route as GPX and your data as JSON.

Privacy, plainly

Your trips never
leave your iPhone.

No accounts. No analytics. No developer servers. Your trips, stops and photos live on your device. The only thing that touches the network is Apple Maps, drawing the map and looking up place names. Read the privacy notes.
